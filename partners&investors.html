our current client

Aradel Holdings

Aradel Holdings Plc is Nigeria's foremost integrated independent energy company, delivering sustainable oil and gas solutions since 2005. With a production capacity of 13,250 barrels of crude oil per day (103% YoY growth) and 6,237 BOE/d of gas, Aradel operates four oil fields (OML 54, 34, 53, OPL 227) and an 11,000 bbl/d modular refinery that contributes 50% of group revenue. The company recently acquired Shell's onshore assets via the Renaissance consortium and completed the $19.5M purchase of Olo/Olo West marginal fields from TotalEnergies/NNPC.

Aradel exemplifies indigenous innovation with Nigeria's first zero-flare gas plant (100MMscf/d capacity), pioneering modular refining, and a N3 trillion NGX listing - the largest oil/gas stock on the exchange. Their 2600% share price growth reflects strategic investments in upstream production, midstream refining, and gas monetization while maintaining 100% Nigerian workforce and local content leadership.
