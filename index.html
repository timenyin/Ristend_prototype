Welcome
to our Risten

Risten Development Company Nigeria Limited is a trusted indigenous oil and gas service provider delivering Well Engineering, Asset Integrity Management, and Production Optimization solutions across Nigeria and the Gulf of Guinea.

With 500+ successful projects, 98% client satisfaction, and 30+ years of regional expertise, we combine cutting-edge technology with hands-on experience to maximize operational efficiency, ensure safety, and reduce costs. Clients choose us for our proven results, certified professionals, and tailored solutions that tackle the industry's toughest challenges.

What we Do

Delivering Industrial Excellence Through Innovation

Custom Pressure Vessel Fabrication

Precision-engineered for harsh industrial environments

We design and manufacture ASME-certified pressure vessels for oil/gas, chemical, and power sectors. Our vessels comply with international safety standards, featuring corrosion-resistant materials and advanced welding techniques to ensure longevity in high-pressure operations.

Electrostatic Crude Oil Treaters

Maximize production efficiency with clean oil output
Our custom electrostatic treaters remove water and solids from crude oil streams, optimizing quality for pipelines or refineries. Built with fail-safe electrical systems and automated controls, they reduce downtime while meeting export specifications.
