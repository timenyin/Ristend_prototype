About Risten

We Have Built An Enviable Reputation

For over 30 years, Risten Development Company Nigeria Limited has been the trusted partner for Well Engineering and Oilfield Solutions across Nigeria and the Gulf of Guinea. Our commitment to excellence has delivered 500+ successful projects with a 98% client satisfaction rate, establishing us as leaders in precision drilling, asset integrity, and rapid production services.

Risten is certified to international QHSE standards, ensuring the highest quality in all well engineering and production optimization services.

“ Every project begins with a commitment to safety, efficiency, and client success.“
We combine cutting-edge technology with unmatched regional expertise to solve the oil & gas industry’s most complex challenges. From well design to crude evacuation.
What we do

Best in Well Engineering & Oilfield Services

With 30+ years of experience, Risten Development delivers specialized oilfield services across Nigeria and the Gulf of Guinea. We provide end-to-end solutions in Well Engineering, Asset Integrity, and Production Optimization through certified expertise and innovative technologies.

Our Methodology

Well Engineering Excellence

  • How do we assemble Nigeria's top Well Engineering talent?
    We recruit only certified professionals with 10+ years field experience, including veterans from major operators like Shell and Chevron. Our teams undergo rigorous QHSE training and continuous skills development to maintain cutting-edge expertise in drilling optimization and completions.
  • How to partner with us for your oilfield project?
    Submit your project requirements through our portal or contact our business development team directly. We conduct free preliminary assessments within 72 hours, followed by customized proposals with transparent pricing and timelines.
  • What qualifies vendors for our supply chain?
    We vet all suppliers through ISO 9001 and API Q1 compliance checks, on-site audits, and performance trials. Preferred vendors gain access to our 500+ project network across the Gulf of Guinea.
